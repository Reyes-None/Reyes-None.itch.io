What You're Looking For

Evidence Types

Every ghost leaves exactly 3 of 8 possible evidence types. Find them, mark your journal, and cross-reference until one ghost remains.

📡EMF Level 5
Your EMF Reader spikes to Level 5 near ghost interactions. Place it passively in the ghost room — it collects evidence hands-free while you work on others.
🎙️Spirit Box
Hold the Spirit Box in the ghost's room and ask it questions. A response in a distorted voice confirms this evidence. Never use near a Ghoul.
❄️Freezing Temps
Thermometer reads below 0°C in the ghost room. This is also how you find the ghost room in the first place — scan every room on arrival.
🔵Ghost Orb
Visible only through the Video Camera or Head-Mounted Camera. A floating orb drifts through the ghost room. Set Graphics to Level 1 for best visibility.
🖐️Handprints
Revealed by the Blacklight (UV). Ghosts leave fingerprints and footprints on surfaces. Drop salt piles to force guaranteed footprints when the ghost walks through.
✍️Ghost Writing
Place the Spirit Book on the floor near activity. Some ghosts write sinister messages in it passively. Leave it in the room and check back — don't hold it.
🔴Laser Grid
Set up the Laser Projector in the ghost room. Ghosts with this evidence become faintly visible when passing through the grid. Film it with a Video Camera.
🌿Wither
Environmental decay — metal rusts, paintings distort, Flower Pot blooms wilt and die over time. Passive and unmistakable, but it takes time. Place the Flower Pot early.

Your Arsenal

Equipment

Starter items are free every job. Purchasable equipment costs in-game money and may require minimum levels. Choose wisely — your carry limit is fixed.

🔦
Flashlight
Starter · Utility
Your basic light source. Electronic — malfunctions near a hunting ghost. Turn it off when hiding. Always carry a backup.
📻
EMF Reader
Starter · Evidence
Detects electromagnetic readings 1–5. Level 5 confirms EMF evidence. Can be placed passively to collect evidence while you focus elsewhere.
🔵
Blacklight (UV)
Starter · Evidence
Reveals ghostly handprints, fingerprints, and footprints. Turn off room lights for best visibility. Pair with salt for reliable footprint detection.
🌡️
Thermometer
Starter · Evidence
Measures room temperature. Below 0°C confirms Freezing Temps. Also your primary method for locating the ghost room — scan every room first.
📷
Photo Camera
Purchasable · Income
Photograph ghost events, interactions, and manifestations for bonus money. A clear photo of the ghost is worth significant cash. Always bring one.
📹
Video Camera
Purchasable · Evidence
Detects Ghost Orbs automatically when placed in the ghost room. Records activity while you're elsewhere. Lower graphics to Level 1 to see orbs clearly.
🎙️
Spirit Box
Purchasable · Evidence
Ask the ghost questions aloud. A distorted voice response confirms Spirit Box evidence. Do NOT use this if you suspect a Ghoul.
📗
Spirit Book
Purchasable · Evidence
Leave it on the floor in the ghost room. Ghosts with Ghost Writing evidence will write in it passively. Don't hold it — just place and check back.
🔴
Laser Projector
Purchasable · Evidence
Projects a laser grid that makes ghosts with Laser Grid evidence faintly visible. Film with a camera. Lower graphics dramatically improve detection.
🎥
Head-Mounted Camera
Purchasable · Evidence
Worn on your head — detects Ghost Orbs passively while you move and use other equipment with both hands free.
🌸
Flower Pot
Purchasable · Evidence
Detects Wither evidence — the flowers wilt and die if the ghost causes environmental decay. Place it early; the timer is random and can be long.
🧂
Salt Canister
Purchasable · Utility
Sprinkle salt piles in the ghost room. A ghost with Handprints evidence leaves a glowing UV footprint in salt — confirms evidence instantly. Also reveals movement.
✝️
Cross
Purchasable · Survival
Placed near the ghost room entrance — prevents the ghost from initiating a hunt within its radius. Critical against Demons that hunt at full sanity.
🕯️
Lighter
Starter · Utility
Lights candles found around job sites. Lit candles slow your sanity drain significantly. Wisps will light candles themselves — watch for this behavior.
🏮
Lantern
Purchasable · Utility
Brighter and longer-range than a flashlight. Not electronic — will not malfunction near a hunting ghost. The safest light source for cautious players.
⚡
Energy Drink
Purchasable · Survival
Restores sprint energy. Critical on large maps and during extended chases. Use just before sprinting for maximum distance.
⌚
Energy Watch
Purchasable · Info
Displays your current sanity level at all times. Without it, you're guessing when hunts will begin. Highly recommended for understanding your real risk.
🫙
Holy Oil
Purchasable · Survival
Place at the ghost room doorway. Prevents certain ghosts (especially The Wisp) from initiating hunts through that entrance. Situationally powerful.
🔫
Rock Salt Shotgun
Purchasable · Combat
Fires rock salt to temporarily repel the ghost mid-hunt. Does not kill — buys you seconds to reach a closet. A last resort, not a strategy.
🪬
Umbra Board
Cursed Object · Communication
Ask "What is your favorite room?" — the planchette spells out the ghost room name. High risk of triggering a hunt. Not available on Nightmare difficulty.
🪞
Haunted Mirror
Cursed Object · Navigation
Reflects a static image of the ghost room. Identify the room from the visual without entering. Safer than the Umbra Board for room location.
🎵
Music Box
Cursed Object · Detection
Wind it and walk through the map. The ghost hums along when you're near its room — louder as you approach. A gentle and relatively safe way to find the room.
🔮

Cursed Object · High Risk
Forces a ghost manifestation — perfect for photos and evidence. Triggers an immediate hunt when used. Only activate with a hiding spot planned in advance.
🪄
Fortune Teller
Cursed Object · Information
Reveals a random piece of ghost information — its room, evidence, or behavior. Can give you a crucial early lead, but red herrings are possible.
Survive Longer

Tips & Strategies

The difference between an experienced investigator and a dead one is almost always preparation and knowledge.

I
🌡️ First Steps
Always Find the Ghost Room First
Start every job by scanning all rooms with the Thermometer. A reading below 0°C locates the ghost room. All other evidence gathering happens there — never skip this step.
II
📓 Evidence
Trust Your Journal
Mark evidence as you find it and scratch out what you've ruled out. The journal narrows down ghost possibilities automatically — keep it updated in real time, not at the end.
III
🙈 Before You Start
Map Your Hiding Spots Immediately
When you enter a map, locate every closet and locker before you start investigating. When a hunt begins you have seconds. Searching during a hunt is how hunters die.
IV
🔦 During Hunts
Turn Off All Electronics When Hiding
A malfunctioning flashlight or Spirit Box broadcasts your location to the ghost. The moment you hear the hunt music, turn everything electronic off before hiding.
V
🧂 Evidence
Salt Forces Handprints Every Time
Drop salt piles in the ghost room doorway. If the ghost has Handprints evidence and walks through salt, it leaves a UV-visible footprint 100% of the time. Reliable and fast.
VI
🎙️ Caution
Be Careful With Spirit Box
The Ghoul hunts the moment you ask it a question via Spirit Box. If you have Freezing and Ghost Orb already, consider ruling out the Ghoul before using it at all.
VII
📷 Money
Always Carry the Photo Camera
Ghost event and manifestation photos are your primary income. A clear photo of the ghost during a manifestation is the most valuable — aim for the ghost, not the aftermath.
VIII
🕯️ Sanity
Light Every Candle You Find
Lit candles significantly slow sanity drain. Sweep the map and light them during safe periods. Lower sanity means more hunts — and some ghosts hunt regardless of sanity level.
IX
👁️ Settings
Lower Graphics for Better Evidence Visibility
Ghost Orbs and Laser Grid evidence are dramatically clearer at Roblox Graphics Level 1. Go to Settings → Graphics Mode (Manual) and drop it all the way down.
X
⏰ Overtime
Overtime Is Not Optional
When the timer hits zero, overtime begins and the ghost becomes aggressively dangerous. If you haven't identified yet, work fast and get out. Do not stay to farm extra photos.
XI
👥 Teamwork
Assign Clear Roles
One hunter scans for the ghost room, one deploys cameras and the Spirit Book, one handles the Spirit Box. Three people doing the same thing is three people wasting time.
XII
🌿 Wither
Deploy the Flower Pot Early
Wither takes time to manifest, and the timer is random. Place the Flower Pot in the ghost room the moment you identify it. Waiting until later may mean never getting this evidence at all.
History & Lore

The Mythology

✦ ── ✦ ── ✦

Demonology Solutions

You work for a company called Demonology Solutions. The Boss — a mysterious figure who never accompanies you on jobs — issues your contracts and provides the tutorial. What the company's true purpose is, and how they know so much about the supernatural, is never explained.

Each investigation is simply called a "job." You are not a hero. You are a contractor. The money you earn determines what equipment you bring next time. Death is a real possibility — and The Boss has never once expressed concern about losing a hunter.

"Use the tools at your disposal to hunt down and identify the evil spirits haunting your jobsite."
— The Boss
The Ghost Types & Their Origins

The entities in Demonology are drawn from real-world folklore, mythology, and legend. The Banshee is a death omen from Irish tradition, said to wail before someone dies. The Wendigo is a cannibalistic spirit from Algonquian folklore — born from starvation and desperation in the wilderness. The Aswang is a shapeshifting monster from Filipino mythology, one of the most feared creatures in its home culture.

Others come from Jewish tradition (the Dybbuk, a possessing spirit of a restless dead soul), Greek mythology (the Keres, spirits of violent and cruel death), and ancient legend (the Leviathan, a primordial sea beast of chaos). The Entity and the Nightmare are unique to the game's world — the most dangerous types, treated in the lore as things that exist outside normal classification.

Black Magic Academy (School)

The School job site's official name is "Black Magic Academy" — BMA. It appears abandoned, yet the structure is intact. Classrooms are set as though students were expected. Lockers line the hallways. The gymnasium still has its equipment in place.

No one at Demonology Solutions explains what happened at BMA, who attended it, or why it's now one of the most haunted locations in their catalog. The developer group is named "Blaqk Magic Blue" — a deliberate echo of the school's name, as if the creators have written themselves into the world.

Ghost Models vs Ghost Types

Regardless of what type of ghost haunts a location, its physical appearance is determined by a separate ghost model — not its type. Models include Abaddon (confirmed female, "The Angel of Death"), Biter (wearing a brown formal suit and tie), Dr. Ratched, Leech, Marrow, Mouthpiece, Rattle, and Watcher.

This means what you see is not what you're hunting. A Phantom could look like Biter. An Entity might manifest as Abaddon. The visual appearance is a mask. Only the evidence tells you the truth.

"The ghost's appearance tells you nothing. The evidence tells you everything."
The Inescapable Prison

The Prison carries a sign at its entrance reading "Inescapable Prison." Unlike homes or a school, it was built to contain things. Long stone hallways, rows of cells, warden quarters, and staff areas suggest a serious facility. What caused the haunting — whether the ghost was once an inmate, a guard, or something drawn to the building's purpose — is left entirely to imagination.

The Asylum

Unlocked only at Level 20, the Asylum is the final and most feared location — a three-story abandoned psychiatric hospital. Cluttered, vandalized, and deeply unsettling. Whatever happened here was sudden enough that patients' belongings remain. Staff records have been disturbed. Isolation cells stand open.

The ghost that haunts the Asylum has had decades to learn its corridors. It knows the hiding spots. It knows where the lights fail. It has been waiting. And now, so are you.
